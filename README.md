##  About Dötbaai
Dötbaai is a dedicated agricultural company committed to providing fresh, organic produce every single day. Based in Juba, South Sudan, our mission is to promote healthy living, support local farmers, and offer sustainable organic farming solutions. With over a decade of experience in the agriculture industry, we strive to make a positive impact on the environment and ensure everyone has access to good, nutritious food.

##  Core Services
We offer a comprehensive suite of agricultural services to ensure crops thrive from seed to harvest:
- **Field Preparation:** Plowing, mulching, and clearing fields.
- **Planting & Maintenance:** Seeding, planting, fertilizing, mowing, and watering/irrigation.
- **Harvest & Distribution:** Professional harvesting and selling of fresh, locally grown vegetables.

## Featured Products
We cultivate and sell a wide variety of fresh, high-quality vegetables at competitive prices, including:
- Irish Potatoes
- Sweet Potatoes
- Yams
- Cassava
- Tomatoes

## Why Choose Us?
- **Sustainability:** We use organic farming practices that promote soil health, biodiversity, and reduce pollution.
- **Experience:** Over 10 years of excellence and reliability in the agricultural sector.
- **Community First:** We focus on making fresh food accessible to all while heavily supporting the local farming community.
